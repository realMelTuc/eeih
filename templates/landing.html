// How It Works
Log, analyze, optimize your route

EEIH turns your exploration sessions into data. No EVE SSO required — just log your runs and let the analytics do the work.

1
Log a Run
After each site, open EEIH and log the type, region, system, difficulty, and loot items with prices from Janice.
2
Enter Loot
Add each item with quantity and unit price. EEIH calculates the total automatically and saves it to your run history.
3
Compare Regions
Visit the Regions view to see which areas produce the best ISK per run, most consistent returns, and highest single-run values.
4
Optimize Routes
Use the loot analysis to understand which item categories drive your ISK — and focus your routes on what pays most.
